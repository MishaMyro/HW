Example test

Present Perfect Test

Complete the tasks to check how well you understand the Present Perfect tense.

Quick reminder

Use have / has + past participle when a past action is connected to the present.

Subject + have / has + past participle

Part 1: Choose the correct answer

  1. She _____ her homework already.
    a) finished b) has finished c) have finished
  2. I _____ never been to Canada.
    a) has b) have c) did
  3. They _____ just arrived.
    a) have b) has c) are

Part 2: Complete the sentences

  1. I __________ (lose) my keys.
  2. He __________ (not finish) the test yet.
  3. We __________ (know) each other for three years.

Part 3: Write your own answers

Write four true sentences about your life. Use the Present Perfect.

  1. I have ________________________________.
  2. I have never __________________________.
  3. I have already ________________________.
  4. I have not _____________________ yet.
