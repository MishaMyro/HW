Be used to + -ing

We use be used to + noun / -ing to say something feels normal or familiar. It can describe a habit, routine, or situation that does not feel strange anymore.

I am used to waking up early. She is used to the noise.

Form

be + used to + noun / -ing

Examples: I am used to homework. and I am used to studying at night.

Meaning

It means the action or situation is normal for you. It is not the same as used to for past habits.

Quick examples

  • I am used to writing English homework.
  • He is used to reading every evening.
  • They are used to working together.

Practice
